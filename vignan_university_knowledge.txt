ABOUT THE UNIVERSITY
---------------------
Vignan's Foundation for Science, Technology and Research (VFSTR) is a deemed-to-be
university located in Vadlamudi, Guntur District, Andhra Pradesh, India.
It was established in 1997 and received the status of Deemed University in 2008
under Section 3 of the UGC Act, 1956.
The university is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC), India
and is approved by AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education).
NAAC Accreditation: 'A' Grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council.
The campus spans over 110 acres in Vadlamudi, near Guntur city.

ATTENDANCE POLICY
------------------
- Minimum attendance required: 75% in each subject per semester
- Students with attendance below 75% are NOT eligible to appear for semester exams
- Medical leave: Students must submit a medical certificate within 3 days of return
- Parents/guardians are notified via SMS if attendance falls below 80%
- Attendance is recorded every class by the faculty using the digital attendance system
- Leave application must be submitted in advance for planned absences
- A student absent for more than 15 consecutive days without notice may be removed from rolls
- Attendance portal: Students and parents can check attendance online via the university ERP portal

EXAMINATIONS AND ASSESSMENT
------------------------------
Examination Pattern (B.Tech):
  - Mid-1 Exam: 7th–8th week of each semester (20 marks)
  - Mid-2 Exam: 14th–15th week of each semester (20 marks)
  - End Semester Exam (ESE): After completion of semester (60 marks)
  - Total: 100 marks per subject

Internal Assessment:
  - Assignments: 2 per subject per semester
  - Quizzes and class tests contribute to internal marks

Grading System:
  - O (Outstanding): 90–100
  - A+ (Excellent): 80–89
  - A (Very Good): 70–79
  - B+ (Good): 60–69
  - B (Above Average): 50–59
  - C (Average): 40–49
  - F (Fail): Below 40

Supplementary Exams:
  - Students who fail up to 2 subjects may appear in supplementary exams held in June/July
  - Revaluation of answer scripts can be requested within 10 days of result declaration

Exam Halls and Rules:
  - Hall tickets are mandatory to enter the examination hall
  - Mobile phones are strictly prohibited in exam halls
  - Students must carry their university ID card to all exams
